Nmap Hands-on Lab

Beginner

Objective

Identify open ports and services of a target host.

Steps

Step 1: Run a basic scan nmap 192.168.1.1

Step 2: Perform SYN scan nmap -sS 192.168.1.1

Step 3: Detect services nmap -sV 192.168.1.1

Step 4: Scan specific ports nmap -p 21,22,80,443 192.168.1.1

Step 5: Scan entire subnet nmap 192.168.1.0/24

Step 6: OS detection nmap -O 192.168.1.1

Step 7: Use default scripts nmap -sC 192.168.1.1

Step 8: Fast scan (top ports) nmap -F 192.168.1.1

Step 9: Save output in normal format nmap -sV 192.168.1.1 -oN result.txt

Step 10: Save output in XML format nmap -sV 192.168.1.1 -oX result.xml

Tasks

1. Basic scan
2. SYN scan
3. Version detect
